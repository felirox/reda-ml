Robot WANG REDA

This is the team wuhan hunter squad, have developed a conversational robot, which moniters physical distancing and spreads awareness regarding the situation of covid 19 and its post corona life.

Features

I can do the following tasks, to spread the awareness

Moniter Physical Distancing

Our robot, uses YOLO, a cnn based object detection algorithm, to detect humans, and find the physical distance between them

Navigate near to the victims

If physical distancing is not met, the robot will navigate to the person and yell at them to move away

Spread awareness

Robot also communicates with speech input, thus, people can speak with robot. it responds to the user queries

covid 19 awareness analytics

A website where, all the data will be displayed with tools such as bar graph and pie charts, to study the awareness created in human beings
